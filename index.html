About

Jesse Konditi is a creative designer & the FOUNDER/CEO of JESCOD STUDIOS which is a company that specializes in professional photography, video production, graphics designs, web development, all video editing services and professional voice overs.

I am a graduate from The University Of Nairobi, with a bachelor's degree [Second Class Honors-Upper Division] in Procurement and Supply Chain Management. However, I'm currently pursuing a career in the creative industry (which is not related to what I studied in campus). I am a creative designer, video editor/creator, voice artist and photographer.

I love meditating, interacting with new people, traveling, writing, making videos, swimming and taking pictures. I am a thinker and I often ask alot questions.
